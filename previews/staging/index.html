A Wordle-style game that asks you to identify the Bible book from its verse, testament, section, and first-letter clues. Inspired from Bibdle by George Powell & Silent Summit Co.

How to play

Guess the Bible book from the verse. Use the feedback cards to narrow your answer in as few tries as possible.

Gameplay

Guess books

Enter a Bible book name and submit a guess. The puzzle compares your guess with the hidden answer.

Read feedback

Each card shows whether the testament, section, era, and order are correct, close, or wrong.

Finish the round

Daily mode tracks streaks and leaderboard placement. Practice mode lets you keep playing with no streak pressure.
